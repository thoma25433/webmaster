Frequently Asked Questions

What is required to participate in your training programs?

Participants must be at least 18 years old and in good physical health. No prior experience is necessary.

What is included in the training programs?

Our training programs include accommodation, meals, and all course materials. Participants will also have access to our state-of-the-art training facilities and equipment.

What can I expect during a space travel experience?

During a space travel experience, you can expect to participate in various activities such as scientific experiments, space walks, and more. You will also have the opportunity to experience weightlessness and see the Earth from orbit. All necessary equipment and support will be provided.

Is space travel safe?

Space travel carries some inherent risks, but we take every precaution to ensure the safety of our travelers. Our spacecraft and equipment are regularly inspected and maintained, and our team is highly trained and experienced.
