Safety

At Star Shuttle, we understand that space travel requires the highest level of safety. That's why we have made it our top priority to ensure the safety and well-being of our passengers and crew members at all times.

Our spacecrafts are designed and built using state-of-the-art technology and undergo rigorous testing and inspections before each flight. We hired experienced trained pilots and crew members that can adapt to any tough situation.

We also enforce strict rules and procedures to lower the risk of accidents or malfunctions during flight, and before launch including:

  • Check the spaceships fuel and oxygen levels before takeoff.
  • Real-time monitoring and communication between ground control and spacecraft.
  • Strict flight procedures and protocols to ensure the safety of passengers and crew.
  • Emergency oxygen supply, emergency landing procedures and emergency communication.

If an emergency were to happen we have emergency response teams ready to respond any bad situation.

We at Star Shuttle are committed to giving are customers the safest and smoothest space travel experience possible. If you have any questions or concerns about safety make sure to go to the contact page and send us a message.

Thank you for choosing Star Shuttle for your space travel needs. We look forward to making your journey safe and enjoyable.
